About

As a web developer, I enjoy solving problems, making new applications and posing a big impact whenever I go.

Since I was in High School, my interest in coding grew as I participated in Computer Symposiums and interacting with real-time software developers.

I was in Daystar doing a short course in ICDL where I started coding on my own and finally decided to join Moringa in order to boost my knowledge and efficiency in coding. In Moringa, I learned Javascript, specialized in python and taught myself PHP which was a great achievement in a week sprint while developing a real-time application.

I was able to enhance my leadership skills through working as a scrum master in two group projects namely Emay which was an emergency application giving people solutions to various emergencies and also giving users the ability to post emergencies and receive help. The other application was Haki mobile application belonging to KnownAfrique LLP and enables legal e-consult and finding a lawyer. Our part in Haki mobile application was adding more features such as a booking feature for clients and a lawyer schedule making feature with an online mpesa payment system.

For Emay application we were able to meet all application specifications in one week while for Haki mobile application I was able to learn a new language and develop the needed features in two weeks sprint.
